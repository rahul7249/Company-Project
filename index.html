About Us

At EFFINEXUS Technologies, we specialize in developing software for school accounts and academic management. Our key product, the School Accounts & Academic Management System (SAAMS), offers extensive features for managing schools efficiently.

SAAMS Features

Users of the Software:

  • Administrator/Management
  • Principal
  • Account Officer
  • Accountant
  • Teacher
  • Computer Operator
  • Student

Administrator Features

  • Verify other Users
  • Setup Classes, Fees, and Accounts
  • Backup and Restore Data
  • Manage Student Profiles
  • Generate Fee Receipts and Vouchers
  • View Financial Reports

Teacher Features

  • Setup Classes and Subjects
  • Enter Test Marks and Attendance
  • Print Report Cards
  • Send SMS to Students
  • Upload Tutorials, Assignments, and Notices

Student Features

  • View Results, Attendance, and Assignments
  • Pay Fees Online
  • Access School Calendar and Notices
  • Appear in Online Quizzes
